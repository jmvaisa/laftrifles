#	added our own tweaks because of outcomes that seemed needlessly
#	complicated, counterintuitive, or convoluted when converted
#	into standard outcome syntax.  Still, with over 5000
#	outcome descriptions provided, editing this will likely be a
#	gradual process, and we are grateful(?) to Mr. Lovelace for
#	the monumental effort it took to get the ball rolling with the
#	mostly useful original file.
